Who We Are

The Ex-NYS Comrades Association (ENYSCA) was established with a singular purpose: to empower former NYS servicemen and women, enabling them to thrive in their post-service lives and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision, Mission and Core Values

  • Vision

    To be a dynamic, united, and progressive organization that champions the personal growth and professional development of its members while playing an active role in Kenya’s development.

  • Mission

    To create a platform that fosters unity, mentorship, and support, ensuring that every former NYS member is equipped with the tools, networks, and opportunities needed to achieve success in their civilian lives.

Our Mandate

At ENYSCA, we are committed to empowering former NYS servicemen and women, creating opportunities for their growth, and contributing to Kenya's national development.

Empowerment of Ex-NYS Discharges

Provide opportunities for professional development, career growth, and social reintegration for discharged NYS members.

Networking and Employment Support

Act as a liaison between members and employers, facilitating job opportunities and leveraging skills acquired during service.

Mentorship and Guidance

Mentor younger members transitioning from service to civilian life, offering advice and support.

Social and Professional Support

Create platforms for members to maintain social connections, share experiences, and access emotional and psychological support.

Advocacy and Representation

Advocate for the interests of ex-NYS members in both governmental and private sectors, ensuring their contributions are recognized and valued.

National Development

Actively participate in initiatives that leverage members’ expertise and skills to support Kenya’s growth and progress..

Notable Members

Edward Osore, Coy 1980

Retired Assistant Director NYS.

Beginning as a recruit to a serviceman and rising to Assistant Director, my NYS journey was marked by discipline, resilience, and a commitment to excellence. The service taught me the value of hard work and dedication, shaping my leadership and career in public service. These principles remain a guiding light, reminding me of the importance of service to the nation. ENYSCA ensures these timeless values continue to guide and connect us as we contribute to Kenya's progress.

John Okumu, Coy 1988

Chief Principal NYS Engineering Institute

My time in the NYS instilled discipline, resilience, and a deep sense of community, values that continue to shape my life and career. As a proud member of EX-Serviceman, I have witnessed the incredible impact of these principles in fostering personal growth and nation-building. Together, we can uphold this legacy and inspire future generations to do the same.

Eng Paul Langat, Coy 2002

Engineer, Kenya Airways

My time in the NYS, especially as the chairman of the Catholic Association, was instrumental in shaping my character and values. The discipline instilled through service, combined with the spiritual foundation of faith and Christian principles, taught me the importance of integrity, dedication, and selfless service. ENYSCA continues to be a vital platform for connecting with fellow comrades and upholding these values, ensuring that the principles learned in the service remain a guiding light in both my career and personal life.
